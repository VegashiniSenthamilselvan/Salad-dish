Vegan Salad



A vegan salad is a plant-based dish that excludes any animal products, including meat, dairy, and eggs. These salads are rich in nutrients and can be incredibly diverse, featuring a variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, grains, nuts, seeds, and legumes. Vegan salads are not only healthy and vibrant but also offer a wide range of flavors and textures, making them a versatile option for meals.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens: 4 cups (e.g., spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • Cherry tomatoes: 1 cup, halved
  • Cucumber: 1, sliced
  • Red bell pepper: 1, diced
  • Carrots: 2, shredded
  • Red onion: 1/4, thinly sliced
  • Avocado: 1, diced
  • Chickpeas: 1 can (15 oz), drained and rinsed
  • Sunflower seeds: 1/4 cup
  • Dried cranberries: 1/4 cup
  • Fresh herbs: 1/4 cup, chopped (e.g., parsley, cilantro, or basil)li>
  • Olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Dijon mustard: 1 tsp
  • Maple syrup: 1 tsp
  • Salt and pepper: to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens. Halve the cherry tomatoes. Slice the cucumber and red bell pepper. Shred the carrots. Thinly slice the red onion. Dice the avocado.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, shredded carrots, and red onion.
  3. Add the chickpeas, sunflower seeds, dried cranberries, and fresh herbs.
  4. Gently fold in the diced avocado to avoid mashing it.
  5. Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad.
  6. Toss gently to ensure all ingredients are coated with the dressing.
  7. Serve the salad immediately as a main dish or a side.
